SOME THINGS ABOUT ME

Hi, my name is Dom Starkey. I am an experienced software developer, specialising in front-end technologies, with a background in engineering, technology and science. I have hands-on experience building successful software and hardware projects as well as creating and maintaining complex cloud-based applications. I enjoy working on exciting new projects with fast turn-around times that allow me to utilise and expand my skills.

Over the last four years, I've worked in integrated software teams working on a number of long-term projects. I have presented at trade meetings, worked with hardware engineers and suppliers in the far east and I've helped to build teams from scratch to support multi-million pound software applications.
